Chungking Express

HOME CAST REVIEW

REVIEW

Moustapha Sabaly

February 5, 2023

★★★★★

This film tells two stories, similar in their theme of loneliness, pain and fate, all shot in a breathless color and hand-held camera work to create a beautiful view of Hong Kong in the 1990s.

Amazing dreamlike soundtrack to get you immersed in something that feels like a dream. Heartwarming yet sad.

I can only give this movie so much credit. Even while looking great and just being an overall wonderful movie, The writing also stands out and has little tidbits that rock. The "May 1" can idea, with the connection between birthdays and expiration... so clever.

Chungking Express Trivia

  • Since Chungking Express was filmed in sequence or "like a road movie" as Kar-Wai Wong has said, Wong wrote each scene either the night before or in the morning of the day of filming.
  • Quentin Tarantino signed a deal with Miramax specially to start his own releasing company so that he could get Chungking Express (1994) out to a wider public.
  • Faye Wong is better known in Hong Kong as a pop singer. Indeed, that is her singing on the cover version of The Cranberries song "Dreams" that we hear during the film.
